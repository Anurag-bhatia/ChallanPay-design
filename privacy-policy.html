Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

I. Background

This Privacy Policy outlines how Sproutech Solutions Private Limited ("we," "us," or "our"), operating ChallanPay, collects, uses, and protects your data when you access our challan payment platform.

Company Details:

  • Operator: Sproutech Solutions Private Limited
  • Location: India Accelerator Coworking, Lower Ground Floor, LG-007-02, MGF Metropolis Mall, MG Road, Gurugram, Haryana, 122002
  • CIN: U74900DL2015PTC285360

Purpose: Our platform facilitates online settlement of traffic challans through courts and Lok Adalats.

Policy updates take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

II. Information Collection

We collect several categories of data to provide and improve our services:

Personal Data

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Vehicle registration details
  • Driver's license information (when applicable)

Log Data

  • IP addresses
  • Geolocation information
  • Browser specifications and version
  • Device identifiers
  • Page visit analytics and browsing patterns
  • Language preferences
  • Time zone settings

Payment Data

  • Billing details and transaction information
  • Payment processing through third-party processors (Razorpay, Paytm)

IMPORTANT: Card numbers and banking information are NOT retained by ChallanPay. All payment data is securely processed by our payment partners.

Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and web beacons for:

  • Security monitoring
  • Fraud prevention
  • User experience enhancement
  • Analytics and performance tracking

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

III. Information Sharing

Your data may be disclosed to the following entities:

Government and Legal Entities

  • Courts and judicial authorities
  • Lok Adalats
  • Government agencies and regulatory bodies
  • Law enforcement upon legal demand

Business Partners

  • Service partners under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Affiliates and subsidiaries
  • Investors (under non-disclosure agreements)
  • Marketing associates (with consent)

Business Transitions

In the event of mergers, acquisitions, or business transfers, your information may be disclosed to business successors.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose information when required by court orders, regulatory demands, or to comply with applicable laws.

IV. Data Usage Purposes

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Identity verification and authentication
  • Processing challan payments and contestations
  • Service request fulfillment
  • Transaction processing and confirmation

Customer Support

  • Responding to inquiries and support requests
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Providing assistance with challan resolution

Communications

  • Transaction updates and status notifications
  • Service announcements
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Educational content about traffic rules

Business Operations

  • Operational management and efficiency
  • Service improvement and feature development
  • User experience personalization
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

Legal and Security

  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Security monitoring and threat prevention
  • Dispute resolution

V. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You can request details about the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You can update or correct inaccurate or incomplete data by contacting us.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to:

  • Legal obligations requiring retention
  • Ongoing service commitments
  • Regulatory compliance needs
  • Dispute resolution requirements

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can revoke previously granted permissions for data processing, particularly for marketing communications.

Grievance Redressal

For any concerns or complaints regarding data handling, please contact our support team.

VI. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

Standard Retention

  • Information retained as long as needed for stated purposes
  • Up to 3 years post-service completion

Extended Retention

Data may be retained longer when:

  • Required by law or regulation
  • Necessary for ongoing legal proceedings
  • Needed for financial record-keeping
  • Essential for dispute resolution

Deletion Requests

Deletion requests are considered on a case-by-case basis, subject to regulatory constraints and legal obligations.

To request data deletion, please contact our support team at challan.support@lawyered.in

Security Measures

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Secure payment processing through certified partners

Liability Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: While we implement robust security measures, we disclaim liability for:

  • Unauthorized access by third parties
  • Hacking incidents or cyber attacks
  • Data loss due to circumstances beyond our control
  • Security breaches resulting from user negligence

User Responsibility: You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for all activities under your account.

VIII. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our platform uses various tracking technologies:

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for platform functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns and performance
  • Marketing Cookies: Enable personalized advertising
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings and preferences
  • Third-party cookie management tools
  • Opt-out mechanisms provided by advertising partners

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality and user experience.

Our platform integrates with third-party services:

Payment Processors

  • Paytm

We use third-party analytics tools to understand user behavior and improve our services.

Important Notice

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before sharing information.

X. Children's Privacy

ChallanPay services are intended for users aged 18 and above.

  • We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18
  • If we discover information from a minor, we will delete it promptly
  • Parents or guardians should contact us if they believe a minor has provided information

XI. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed and stored in India. However:

  • Some service providers may be located outside India
  • Data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws
  • Adequate safeguards are in place for international transfers

XII. Marketing Communications

By using ChallanPay, you consent to receive communications via:

  • SMS messages
  • WhatsApp notifications
  • Email correspondence
  • In-app notifications
  • Push notifications (if enabled)

Types of Communications

  • Transactional updates (cannot be opted out)
  • Service announcements
  • Promotional offers and features
  • Educational content about traffic rules

Opt-Out Options

You can opt-out of promotional communications by:

  • Clicking unsubscribe links in emails
  • Replying STOP to SMS messages
  • Contacting our support team

XIII. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time.

Notification of Changes

  • Policy modifications will be posted on this page
  • The effective date will be updated accordingly
  • Significant changes may be communicated via email or platform notifications

Acceptance of Changes

Continued use of the platform after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.

User Responsibility: We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

  • Notifications will be sent via registered email or phone

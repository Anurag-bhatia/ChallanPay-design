Process & Timeline

Our legal team typically reviews cases within 2-3 business days. Complex cases may take up to 7-10 days. You'll receive updates via email and SMS throughout the process.

After payment, our legal team files the e-challan with the traffic department. You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours. Our lawyers then prepare your defense for court proceedings.

In most cases, our lawyers can represent you in court. However, the magistrate may require your personal presence for certain types of violations. We'll inform you in advance if required.

Support & Contact
